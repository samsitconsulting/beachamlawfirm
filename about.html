Frederick Arnold Beacham, Jr. is a solo practitioner in downtown Lexington, South Carolina since opening his law firm in 2005. He earned his Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of South Carolina and was an honor graduate of the University Of South Carolina School Of Law. As a former South Carolina state prosecutor, Arnold has experience that provides him with a unique perspective in defending criminal cases. He regularly handles cases ranging from traffic offenses, DUIs, gun and drug charges, CDVs, automobile accidents, and more.

Arnold is a member of the South Carolina Bar, Lexington County Bar, and Richland County Bar Associations. He has represented clients throughout South Carolina in Circuit, Magistrate and Municipal Courts, and the United States District Court for South Carolina. Arnold is married and has two sons. He coaches recreational soccer and plays recreation basketball with a league. Arnold also enjoys hunting, fishing, skiing, flying model airplanes, and playing guitar. Arnold is well known for his signature BBQ sauce.
